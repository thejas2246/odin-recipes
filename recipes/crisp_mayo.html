Chili Crisp Mayo

This chili crisp may has just two ingredients. With your favorite mayo and a spoonful (or two) of chili crisp, you can whip up a flavorful topping that adds a delicious kick and a bit of texture to almost anything. Use it on sandwiches, burgers, seared tuna, or use as a dipping sauce for grilled chicken. For extra spicy, add an additional tablespoon of chili crisp.
